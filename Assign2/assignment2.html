Websites about Protected Areas Issues

Today, one third of land in the world is degraded by human activities. People transform their activities from production to forestry or landscapes that harms biodiversity and jeopardizes of ecosystem services like carbon storage. Protected areas provides solution that allows such places to have a safeguard nature and cultural resources to protect human beings and animal. Moreover, if the protected areas is well managed and fairly governed, the livelihoods of the ecosystem will be sustainable development for the world. (IUCN, 2022)

6 websites covering related issues and strategies.

  • IUCN works to protect ecosystems, promote the sustainable use of landscapes and advance justice and equity in conversation

  • WWF - Several problems with the world's current protected areas mean that the full diversity of the Earth's habitats and species is not properly protected.

  • Government of Canada - Protected areas - National parks, Migratory Bird Sanctuaries, National Wildlife Areas and areas of marine protection.

  • Toronto and Region Conservation Authority - SCARBOROUGH WATERFRONT PROJECT - Protecting a Natural Wonder on Toronto's Doorstep

  • Canadian Parks and Wilderness Society - Canada has committed to protecting 25% of its land and freshwater by 2025, on the way to 30% by 2030. It is essential to meet these targets to ultimately protect at least half of Canada, which is necessary to conserve the country's natural landscapes and unique wildlife.

  • Convention on Biological Diversity - Protected Areas - The CBD Programme of Work on Protected Areas and progress in its implementation
